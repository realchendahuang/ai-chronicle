whatHappened: 测试让一名提问者仅通过文字与人和机器交谈，再判断哪一方是机器。图灵同时讨论了学习机器、常见反对意见，并预估训练而非手写全部规则可能成为可行路线。
whatHappenedEn: A judge would converse by text with a human and a machine, then decide which was which. Turing also discussed learning machines, common objections, and the possibility of training systems instead of writing every rule by hand.
whyImportant: 它没有解决智能的定义，却给 AI 留下了一个可操作的问题，也长期影响了聊天机器人、人机评测以及公众理解机器智能的方式。
whyImportantEn: The paper did not define intelligence, but it supplied an operational question that shaped chatbots, human evaluation, and public discussion of machine intelligence for decades.
impact:

impactEn:
  industry: The Turing test became the best-known public reference for machine intelligence while prompting lasting debate over whether evaluation should reward imitation of humans.
