On 23 April 2024, SenseTime’s tech day centered on SenseNova 5.0. SenseNova was not new to chat, but a fifth generation performs a declaration: general models need generations—like phones, chips, and the version trains vision firms already knew.

Materials spoke of knowledge, math, reasoning, code, MoE and longer context, cloud-to-edge. Numbers largely come from own evals and need borders. Historical seat is posture: a boxes-and-faces company must hand over industrializable chat and multimodal menus, or markets and buyers will lock it in old labels.

Cloud-to-edge matrices are engineering and sales language at once. For government and enterprise they mean privatization and edge options; for the public they mean SenseTime no longer wants only a security epitaph. 5.0 is a midpoint, not an end—higher generations and harder price wars still wait.
