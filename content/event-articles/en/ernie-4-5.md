On 16 March 2025, Baidu did not flood the stage with lights. ERNIE 4.5 and ERNIE X1 appeared in ERNIE Bot and cloud consoles more like a product changelog: one name for native multimodality, one for deep thinking, both free.

The timing is hard to ignore. After R1, domestic users remeasured every gateway with two rulers—reasoning and price. Baidu held search distribution and Paddle, yet had gone quiet in the open-weight story. The 4.5 plus X1 package answered in product language: flagships can be free; reasoning can be its own tier; an open timeline can follow—later 4.5 open releases cashed part of that sentence into files.

For ordinary users the change is at the doorway: switch capability inside one chat without first learning MoE and RL. For enterprises, Qianfan model IDs mean another integration pass. For the industry, it means a giant’s weapons include daily active users and free strategy, not only papers.

No keynote does not mean no historical seat. Sometimes the seat is simply this: one morning, two model names become buttons, and they do not charge.
