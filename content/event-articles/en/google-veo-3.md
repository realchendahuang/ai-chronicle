In the 2025 I/O cycle, Veo 3 wrote sound into default capability: dialogue, effects, and ambience with the picture. Sora 2 had already habituated the industry to A/V frames; Google spread the same standard through Gemini doors and APIs.

Silent clips suddenly felt half-finished. Post-dub still has value, but “does the model emit sound itself” became a procurement checkbox. Regional and count limits remain; demos stay cleaner than queues.

A/V unity also raised abuse and copyright sensitivity: audio deepfakes feel truer than silent cuts. Veo 3’s seat is a technical step and a governance burden step.
