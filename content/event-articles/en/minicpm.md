Around February 2024, when MiniCPM weights began circulating, people still defaulted to 7B and 70B when asking whether a model could do real work. ModelBest and OpenBMB offered another sentence: small parameters, but Chinese and instruction skill must score on-device.

On-device is not romance; it is constraint—memory, battery, latency, privacy. MiniCPM’s seat is writing those constraints into open checkpoints rather than only closed app models. MiniCPM-V later pushed vision and OCR into the same “pocket cannon” story; edge multimodality stopped being only a slogan.

It did not end cloud races. It only added a column to buy and build lists: can it run locally. Once the column exists, phone, PC, and industry-gateway model stories must answer it.
