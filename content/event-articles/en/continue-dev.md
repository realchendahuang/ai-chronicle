Continue put chat and completion into VS Code/JetBrains sidebars and insisted models swap and flows configure. While Cursor told the future as a fused IDE, Continue told another: keep your editor; choose your model.

Open source lets enterprises audit and self-host. Capability swings with backend models; product philosophy nails “no lock-in.” Acquisition by Cursor around 2026 proved the open sidebar mattered enough to buy, not ignore.

On the agent-coding map, Continue is the editor’s open layer. Without it, commercial IDEs lack one axis of contrast.
