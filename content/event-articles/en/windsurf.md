In November 2024, Windsurf rewrote Codeium’s story: not only a free Copilot alternative, but an AI-native editor with Cascade agents. The category’s second-strong narrative needed a name—Windsurf was that name.

What fights Cursor is the primary UI, not a sidebar plugin. Flows, context, and multi-file edits become default paths. Developers gain a migration option; markets gain comparable pricing and agent styles.

Terminals hold Aider/Claude Code/Codex CLI; editors hold Cursor/Windsurf; open sidebars hold Continue. Agent-coding density is these cells existing together, not one cell declaring endgame.
