On 26 April 2026, Sora web and app experiences discontinued. Help-doc tone was calm: export, timelines, API sunset later. For many users this was more concrete than any new model release—the home-screen icon no longer opened a world.

Shutdown does not negate the 2024 demo or 2025 Sora 2. It adjudicates product form: whether high-cost generation, abuse governance, and platform strategy still point the same way. Seedance copyright war in the same season reminded everyone that external constraints are not only compute bills.

The chronicle should keep both once-a-flagship-door and doors-can-close. Sunset risk on procurement sheets is no longer abstract. Generative video continues; Sora consumer-door cell is struck through on this day.
